# Careers at Materialize

## Join Us in Building the Future of Real-Time Data

At Materialize, we're not just building software – we're reimagining how the world works with data. Every day, our team of engineers, product managers, and business professionals tackles some of the most challenging problems in distributed systems and data infrastructure.

Based in our modern office in New York City's Financial District, we're creating technology that powers real-time decision making for companies around the world. If you're passionate about solving hard problems, working with brilliant colleagues, and building technology that matters, we'd love to meet you.

### Why Work at Materialize

**World-Class Technical Challenges**
Working at Materialize means tackling problems that few companies in the world are equipped to solve. Our engineering challenges span distributed systems, query optimization, storage engines, and real-time data processing at scale. You'll work alongside some of the brightest minds in the industry, many of whom previously built foundational systems at Google, Amazon, Facebook, and other leading technology companies.

**Meaningful Impact**
The technology we're building doesn't just make queries run faster – it fundamentally changes how organizations make decisions. Our customers use Materialize to detect fraud in real-time, optimize supply chains as conditions change, and deliver personalized experiences at the moment of interaction. Your work directly enables companies to be more responsive, more efficient, and more competitive.

**Growth and Learning**
The streaming database space is evolving rapidly, which means there's always something new to learn. We invest heavily in our team's professional development through conference attendance, internal tech talks, research collaborations, and dedicated learning time. Many of our engineers have published papers, spoken at major conferences, and become recognized experts in their fields while working here.

**Collaborative Culture**
We believe the best ideas come from collaboration between diverse perspectives. Our culture emphasizes respectful debate, continuous learning, and shared ownership of outcomes. Whether you're a senior engineer proposing a new architecture or a recent graduate questioning an established pattern, your voice matters.

**Competitive Compensation and Equity**
As a Series C company with significant venture backing, we offer competitive salaries, meaningful equity participation, and comprehensive benefits. Our compensation philosophy is simple: attract and retain the best talent by ensuring everyone is fairly rewarded for their contributions.

### Our Values

**Technical Excellence**: We build systems that are not just fast, but correct, maintainable, and elegant. We take pride in solving hard problems the right way, even when the easy way might work in the short term.

**Customer Obsession**: Every decision we make starts with understanding our customers' needs. We regularly talk to users, analyze usage patterns, and prioritize features that deliver real value.

**Intellectual Honesty**: We admit when we don't know something, acknowledge when we make mistakes, and change our minds when presented with better information. Ego has no place in building great software.

**Inclusive Excellence**: We believe diverse teams build better products. We actively work to create an environment where everyone can do their best work, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

**Long-Term Thinking**: We're building infrastructure that companies will depend on for years to come. We make architectural decisions with longevity in mind and invest in foundational work that pays dividends over time.

### Application Process

Our interview process is designed to be thorough but respectful of your time. For engineering roles, you'll typically go through:

1. **Initial Screen**: 30-minute conversation with our recruiting team
2. **Technical Phone Screen**: 45-minute technical discussion with an engineer
3. **Onsite Interview**: Half-day session including technical deep-dives, system design, and team fit conversations
4. **Reference Checks**: Brief conversations with previous colleagues

We're committed to providing feedback at every stage and accommodating different interview preferences when possible.
