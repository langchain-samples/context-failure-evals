# Press Coverage

## Recent Press Mentions and Coverage

### TechCrunch: "Materialize raises Series C to expand streaming database platform"
*October 15, 2023*

Materialize, the streaming SQL database company, announced a Series C round led by Index Ventures. The New York-based startup plans to use the funding to expand internationally and enhance its cloud platform. CEO Nate Stewart said the company has seen 300% year-over-year growth as enterprises adopt real-time analytics.

### VentureBeat: "Why streaming databases are the future of real-time analytics"  
*August 22, 2023*

In a comprehensive analysis of the streaming database market, VentureBeat highlighted Materialize as a leader in making real-time analytics accessible to mainstream developers. The article praised the company's approach of using familiar SQL syntax for streaming data processing, calling it "a game-changer for organizations that need to act on data in real-time." The piece noted Materialize's growing customer base and strong technical team as key competitive advantages.

### The Information: "Database startups challenge incumbents with real-time capabilities"
*June 8, 2023*

The Information's deep dive into next-generation database companies featured Materialize prominently, highlighting the company's unique approach to streaming SQL. The article noted that Materialize's technology addresses a "massive market need" as companies generate more real-time data than ever before. CTO Alex Chen was quoted extensively about the technical challenges of building strongly consistent streaming databases.

### Forbes: "The Next Wave of Data Infrastructure Startups"
*March 14, 2023*  

Forbes included Materialize in its roundup of promising data infrastructure companies, noting the company's rapid growth. The article highlighted CEO Nate Stewart's background at Confluent and LinkedIn as key to the company's success in the competitive streaming data market. The piece also mentioned Materialize's expansion to offices in New York as signs of the company's scaling efforts.

### InfoWorld: "Materialize Cloud brings streaming SQL to the masses"
*September 12, 2023*

InfoWorld's review of Materialize Cloud praised the platform's ease of use and powerful streaming capabilities. The review highlighted the service's PostgreSQL compatibility and automatic scaling features as major advantages over competing solutions. "Materialize has managed to make streaming data feel as natural as querying a traditional database," the review concluded, giving the platform 4.5 out of 5 stars.

### Database Trends and Applications: "CEO Spotlight: Nate Stewart on the evolution of streaming databases"
*July 19, 2023*

In an extensive interview, CEO Nate Stewart discussed Materialize's vision for making real-time analytics mainstream. Stewart explained how the company's differential dataflow technology enables strong consistency guarantees while maintaining high performance. He also discussed plans for expanding the engineering team at their New York headquarters.

### ZDNet: "Real-time analytics: Moving beyond batch processing"
*April 3, 2023*

ZDNet's analysis of the real-time analytics market positioned Materialize as a key innovator in streaming database technology. The article noted the company's success in attracting enterprise customers who need to process millions of events per second with SQL-level simplicity. The piece highlighted customer case studies showing significant improvements in decision-making speed and operational efficiency.

### TechTarget: "Streaming databases gain enterprise traction"  
*February 7, 2023*

TechTarget's enterprise IT publication featured Materialize in a story about the growing adoption of streaming databases in large organizations. The article noted that while the company was founded recently in 2019, it has quickly gained credibility with Fortune 500 customers. The piece highlighted Materialize's Series B funding round and plans to expand across New York offices.
