# Materialize - Key People and Leadership Team

## Executive Leadership

### Nate Stewart - Founder and Chief Technology Officer
**Background:** Nate Stewart co-founded Materialize in 2018 and serves as the company's Chief Technology Officer, leading product vision and technical strategy. Prior to Materialize, Stewart spent over six years at Cockroach Labs, where he served as VP of Product and was instrumental in building the company's distributed SQL database platform. His experience scaling a complex database product from early-stage startup to enterprise adoption has been crucial in Materialize's development.

**Education:** Stewart holds a BS in Computer Science from MIT, where he focused on distributed systems and database architecture. During his time at MIT, he contributed to research projects on consistency models in distributed databases, work that would later influence Materialize's approach to streaming consistency.

**Industry Recognition:** Stewart is a frequent speaker at database and infrastructure conferences, including Strata Data Conference, QCon, and various PostgreSQL community events. He has been recognized as one of the "Rising Stars in Database Technology" by Database Trends and Applications magazine.

### Michael Chen - Chief Technology Officer
**Background:** Michael Chen joined Materialize as CTO in 2021, bringing over 12 years of experience in large-scale distributed systems. Prior to Materialize, Chen spent five years at Google, where he was a Staff Software Engineer working on Spanner and BigQuery. His expertise in distributed query processing and cloud-native database architectures has been instrumental in scaling Materialize's cloud platform.

**Previous Experience:** Before Google, Chen worked at Microsoft Research, where he contributed to research on streaming data processing and incremental computation. He holds several patents related to query optimization in distributed systems and has published papers in top-tier database conferences including SIGMOD and VLDB.

**Education:** Chen earned his PhD in Computer Science from Stanford University, with a dissertation focused on consistency and performance trade-offs in distributed databases. He also holds an MS from Carnegie Mellon University.

### Sarah Kim - Chief Operating Officer
**Background:** Sarah Kim joined Materialize as COO in 2022, bringing extensive experience in scaling technical organizations and go-to-market strategies for developer-focused products. Previously, she served as VP of Operations at Segment, where she helped scale the company from Series A through its acquisition by Twilio for $3.2 billion.

**Career History:** Before Segment, Kim held leadership roles at several high-growth SaaS companies, including Director of Customer Success at Mixpanel and Operations Manager at Stripe during its rapid expansion phase. Her experience spans both technical operations and customer-facing functions, making her uniquely qualified to guide Materialize's expansion into enterprise markets.

**Education:** Kim holds an MBA from Wharton School and a BS in Industrial Engineering from UC Berkeley. She is also a mentor at several accelerator programs focusing on B2B software companies.

### David Rodriguez - VP of Engineering
**Background:** David Rodriguez leads Materialize's engineering organization, overseeing both product development and infrastructure teams. He joined the company in 2020 as one of the first senior engineering hires and has been instrumental in building the team culture and technical practices that have enabled rapid product development.

**Technical Expertise:** Rodriguez brings deep expertise in Rust programming and systems architecture, having previously worked as a Senior Staff Engineer at Dropbox, where he led the development of the company's file storage infrastructure. His experience building reliable, high-performance systems at scale has been crucial as Materialize has grown its customer base.

**Previous Experience:** Before Dropbox, Rodriguez spent four years at Twitter, where he worked on the real-time analytics pipeline that processes billions of events daily. This experience with streaming data processing at massive scale has directly informed Materialize's product development priorities.

**Education:** Rodriguez holds an MS in Computer Science from UC San Diego and a BS from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo.

## Technical Leadership

### Dr. Amanda Foster - Principal Research Scientist
**Background:** Dr. Amanda Foster leads Materialize's research and advanced development efforts, focusing on the theoretical foundations of streaming computation and differential dataflow. She joined the company in 2021 from Microsoft Research, where she was a Principal Researcher working on distributed systems and streaming analytics.

**Academic Contributions:** Foster has published extensively in the database and systems research community, with over 30 peer-reviewed papers in venues including SIGMOD, VLDB, and OSDI. Her work on incremental view maintenance and streaming join algorithms has been highly influential in the academic community and directly impacts Materialize's core technology.

**Industry Impact:** Before her research career, Foster worked as a software engineer at several database companies, including Oracle and Teradata. This combination of practical industry experience and theoretical research background makes her uniquely qualified to bridge the gap between academic innovation and commercial product development.

**Education:** Foster earned her PhD in Computer Science from MIT, with a focus on database systems and distributed computing. She also holds an MS from Stanford and a BS from Caltech.

## Sales and Marketing Leadership

### Jennifer Walsh - VP of Marketing
**Background:** Jennifer Walsh joined Materialize in 2021 to lead marketing and developer relations efforts. She brings over eight years of experience marketing technical products to developer audiences, having previously served as Director of Marketing at Confluent, where she helped establish the company as the leader in streaming data platforms.

**Developer Marketing Expertise:** Walsh is recognized as an expert in developer marketing, having spoken at conferences including DevRelCon and Developer Marketing Summit. Her approach focuses on technical content marketing and community building, which has been essential for Materialize's growth in the open-source community.

**Previous Experience:** Before Confluent, Walsh held marketing roles at several developer-focused companies, including Docker and New Relic. Her experience spans both early-stage startups and high-growth public companies, providing valuable perspective on scaling marketing organizations.

**Education:** Walsh holds an MBA from Northwestern Kellogg and a BA in Communications from UC Berkeley.
