# Materialize Benefits & Perks

*Based on employee reviews and reports*

**Overall Benefits Rating:** 4.1/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

---

## Health & Wellness

**Medical Insurance:** 🏥
Premium health insurance with multiple plan options. Company covers 100% of employee premiums and 80% for dependents. Includes comprehensive dental and vision coverage. HSA available with company contribution of $1,500 annually.

**Mental Health Support:** 🧠
Partnership with Modern Health for therapy and coaching services. Unlimited sessions with licensed therapists. Additional wellness stipend of $2,000/year for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness activities.

**Employee Rating:** 4.3/5 - *"Best health benefits I've had at a startup. The mental health support is genuinely useful and not just a checkbox."*

---

## Time Off & Flexibility

**Unlimited PTO:** 🏖️
Truly unlimited vacation policy with minimum 3 weeks encouraged. Employees report actually using 4-5 weeks annually without pushback from management. Company shuts down the week between Christmas and New Year's.

**Remote-First Culture:** 🏠
Fully remote-friendly with home office stipend of $3,000 for equipment and furniture. Co-working space reimbursement up to $400/month for those who prefer external office space.

**Flexible Hours:** ⏰
Core collaboration hours vary by team but generally flexible scheduling. Accommodates different time zones across NYC, Austin, London, and remote employees.

**Employee Rating:** 4.5/5 - *"Actually unlimited PTO that you can use. Management actively encourages time off and doesn't guilt trip about coverage."*

---

## Financial Benefits

**Competitive Salaries:** 💰
Salaries benchmarked against top-tier tech companies. Regular market adjustments and performance-based increases. Transparent salary bands shared during hiring process.

**Equity Program:** 📈
Meaningful equity grants for all employees. Stock option program with 4-year vesting and 1-year cliff. Equity refresh grants based on performance and tenure. Recent 409A valuation suggests significant upside potential.

**401(k) Matching:** 🏦
6% company match, vested immediately. Managed through Guideline with low-fee investment options including ESG and target-date funds.

**Employee Rating:** 4.2/5 - *"Equity feels meaningful unlike other startups I've worked at. The 401k match is generous for a company this size."*

---

## Professional Development

**Learning & Development:** 📚
$3,000 annual budget for conferences, courses, and training. Conference attendance encouraged with additional budget for travel and accommodation. Internal tech talks and lunch-and-learns.

**Equipment & Tools:** 💻
Latest MacBook Pro or high-spec Linux workstation. Multiple monitor setup, ergonomic furniture, and premium software licenses. Upgrade cycle every 2-3 years or as needed.

**Career Growth:** 🚀
Clear advancement paths with regular career development conversations. Internal mobility encouraged between teams. Mentorship programs pairing junior and senior employees.

**Employee Rating:** 3.9/5 - *"Good learning budget and they actually approve conference requests. Still building out formal career frameworks as they scale."*

---

## Family Benefits

**Parental Leave:** 👶
16 weeks paid parental leave for all new parents. Additional 2 weeks for birthing parents. Flexible return options including part-time transition period. Lactation support and pumping rooms in offices.

**Family Support:** 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
$2,000 annual childcare stipend. Elder care support through backup care services. Family-friendly company events and flexible scheduling for school events.

**Employee Rating:** 4.0/5 - *"Parental leave policy is progressive for a startup this size. The childcare stipend actually helps with real expenses."*

---

## Office & Culture

**Modern Offices:** 🏢
Premium office spaces in NYC (SoHo) and Austin (downtown). London co-working arrangement with WeWork. Fully stocked kitchens, quality coffee, and catered lunches 3x/week.

**Team Building:** 🎉
Annual company retreat (recent location: Costa Rica). Quarterly team events and regular happy hours. Budget for team dinners and local meetups for remote employees.

**Commuter Benefits:** 🚊
Pre-tax commuter benefits for subway/bus. Bike-to-work reimbursement and secure bike storage in offices. Parking subsidies where applicable.

**Employee Rating:** 4.2/5 - *"Great office culture when in-person. Remote events are well-organized and don't feel like an afterthought."*

---

## Unique Perks

**Innovation Time:** 💡
Monthly hack days and 20% time for personal projects. Internal innovation grants up to $5,000 for promising ideas. Several employee projects have been integrated into the main product.

**Industry Leadership:** 🎤
Support for external speaking, blogging, and open source contributions. Conference speaking opportunities with company backing. Technical blog with bylines for employee authors.

**Sabbatical Program:** ✈️
After 4 years, eligible for 4-week paid sabbatical in addition to regular PTO. Can be used for travel, personal projects, or volunteer work.

**Employee Rating:** 4.0/5 - *"The hack days are genuinely fun and some projects get real resources. Sabbatical program is forward-thinking for a startup."*
