## Salary Insights

**Equity Information:**
Most employees report receiving 0.1% - 0.8% equity grants depending on role and seniority. Senior engineers and managers typically receive 0.3% - 1.2%. Leadership roles (VP+) receive 2% - 5% equity packages.

**Bonus Structure:**
- Engineering: Typically 10-20% annual bonus based on company performance
- Sales: Commission-heavy with 60-120% of quota achievement expectations
- Other roles: 5-15% discretionary bonuses

**Salary Negotiation:**
Employees report that Materialize is generally willing to negotiate on salary and equity mix, particularly for senior technical roles. Several reports mention successful negotiations for additional equity in lieu of higher base salary.

**Geographic Pay Differences:**
- NYC salaries typically 10-15% higher than Austin
- London salaries roughly equivalent to Austin when accounting for cost of living
- Remote salaries generally aligned with Austin ranges
- No significant pay cuts reported for remote work

**Recent Trends:**
Multiple employees mention salary increases in 2023 to remain competitive with larger tech companies. The company appears to benchmark against both startup and big tech compensation for senior roles.
