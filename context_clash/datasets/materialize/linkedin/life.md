## Where We Work

### San Francisco
Our largest engineering team is based in San Francisco's SOMA neighborhood. The team enjoys an open-plan workspace with dedicated focus pods, a fully-equipped maker space for hardware prototyping, and a rooftop terrace with stunning Bay Area views.

Amenities include:
- Gourmet cafeteria with meals by celebrity chef partners
- State-of-the-art fitness center and yoga studio
- Nap pods and meditation rooms
- Game room with vintage arcade machines
- On-site massage therapy and wellness services
- Pet-friendly environment

### Austin
Our Austin team focuses on operations, sales, and customer success. Located in the trendy East Austin area, the space embodies the city's "Keep Austin Weird" spirit while maintaining our commitment to excellence.

Unique features:
- Live music venue that hosts monthly all-hands meetings
- Authentic BBQ food truck on-site twice weekly
- Collaborative spaces designed by local artists
- Rooftop deck with Hill Country views
- Bike repair station and secure storage for Austin's cycling culture

### Denver
Our Denver team in the RiNo district focuses on AI research and emerging technologies, with PhD researchers driving breakthrough thinking.

Research-focused amenities:
- Whiteboard walls throughout the space
- Private research pods with advanced GPU clusters
- Coffee bar featuring local Denver roasters
- Standing desks and walking meeting paths
- Mountain bike storage for Colorado outdoor enthusiasts
- Ski pass program for team building on the slopes
